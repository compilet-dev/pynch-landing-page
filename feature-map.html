Open Questions — Neel + Khoa

  1. Discovery + DM outreach works for Instagram. The rest isn't automated. Fake it till justified, or build on-demand per customer request?
  2. Creator Portal: not MVP but immediately after. How close to MLP? DietFit team is already drowning in creator questions about process, payments, video status — the pain is live now.
  3. Performance tracking is completely greenfield. No existing analytics infra. How much is MVP-blocking vs. post-launch?
  4. Payment flow model: Pynch holds float, PSP in the middle, or back-to-back invoicing? Big EU compliance implications. Decision needed before architecture locks in.
  5. Campaign Mode needs its own UX design sprint separate from Farm Mode — when does that happen?
  6. UGC campaign metrics: CPV, CPI, ROAS, submission speed, re-submission rate, brief accuracy. What actually matters most to brand customers? Needs customer research.
  7. Creator Portfolio as PLG: could be a standalone free product driving brand-side acquisition. Build before or after Creator Portal?
  8. AI Content QA: simple version for MVP (auto-screen against brief basics) or park entirely? Already have a Gemini gem doing something similar — evaluate before rebuilding.
  9. Buy vs. build for payments infra? Platforms like Deel, Tipalti, Routable already do mass creator payments. Evaluate before building.
